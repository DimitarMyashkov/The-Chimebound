# The Chimebound

## Overview

**The Chimebound** is a first-person psychological horror game centered around an ancient folklore creature known as **The Vetrinnik**—a relentless predator that emerges once every fifty years during the week that ends beneath a Blue Moon.

Driven by obsession and curiosity, the player ventures into a remote forest to uncover the truth behind a legend dismissed by most as a myth. What begins as an investigation gradually descends into a desperate fight for survival as the line between folklore and reality disappears.

## The Legend of the Vetrinnik

The Vetrinnik is a towering, emaciated creature with pitch-black skin stretched tightly over visible bones. Its unnaturally long arms end in razor-sharp claws capable of carving deep marks into trees as it moves through the forest canopy.

The creature's presence is announced by the sound of wind chimes hanging from one of its arms. At first, the chimes can only be heard faintly in the distance. As the Vetrinnik draws closer, they begin to shake violently, producing distorted, almost screaming tones that blend with heavy breathing, guttural growls, and the pounding of the victim's own heartbeat.

Its face is concealed beneath tattered cloth and rusted chains adorned with additional chimes, revealing only a wide, manic grin. Long braided hair hangs from its head, with one massive braid carrying a lantern fueled by the souls of its victims. The lantern is the source of the creature's existence; as long as its flame burns, the Vetrinnik lives.

## Core Horror Pillars

* Folklore-driven psychological horror
* Environmental storytelling and investigation
* Sound-based tension and fear
* Survival during pursuit sequences
* Exploration of hidden underground facilities and lush forests
* Mystery, obsession, and the cost of seeking forbidden truths
